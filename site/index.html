Built for peripheral vision

BorgDock lives at the edge of your screen. No tabs, no notifications to chase, no broken flow.

01 — sidebar

Docked & always there

Slim sidebar hugs your screen edge. Toggle with Ctrl+Shift+P. Your desktop resizes around it.

02 — checks

CI at a glance

Check suites and individual runs shown inline. Expand failures to see parsed logs without leaving your editor.

03 — claude

AI code review

Launch Claude Code on any PR with one click. Reviews land back in the sidebar, grouped by severity.

04 — repos

Multi-repo grouping

Watch as many repos as you need. PRs are grouped, sorted by update time, and filterable by status.

05 — notify

Smart notifications

Floating badge alerts you to failing checks and new review requests. Not another notification firehose.

06 — themes

Light & dark

Follows your system preference or set it manually. Ships with two carefully tuned themes.

Three minutes to set up

1

Install & launch

Download the installer, run it. BorgDock starts in your system tray.

2

Authenticate with GitHub

The setup wizard walks you through creating a personal access token. Paste it in, done.

3

Add your repos

Type owner/repo for each repository you want to watch. BorgDock starts polling immediately.

4

Dock & forget

Hit Ctrl+Shift+P to toggle the sidebar. It's there when you glance, gone when you don't.
